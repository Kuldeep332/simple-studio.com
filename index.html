Simple Studio® is a design studio focused on digital products
that works with companies around the world, helping them
grow and expand their business.

Our

People
& Culture

The studio was founded in 2022 by Guzmán
Barquín & Anita Gutiérrez. Two multidisciplinary
designers with 10 years of experience in digital
products.

We strongly believe in the power of collaboration and the idea of growing together by complementing each other's skills.

We rely on transparency and understand that honest communication is the way to get things right.

We take pride in knowing that we will always focus on the well-being and balance of the team, and we truly enjoy our ability to evolve and adapt along the way.

From Uruguay to Spain

we built Simple Studio® with

a group of people who love what they

do.

We created Simple Studio® believing that there is always a better way to do things. We are very passionate about what we do, and we are also the continuous evolution of the experiences we live.

We value honest design and the processes that drive it. We trust in the collaboration between people and how they complement each other.

Above all, we believe in the power of good design.
